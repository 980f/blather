fore and mainsail tack show as at capstans.

  1. The main and fore masts have 3 halyards, course and lower topsail are fixed yards.
  2. The fore'n'aft sails each have a halyard and they are in the reverse order of the downhauls (opposing pairs) and on the same side of the ship as the downhaul.
  3. Arm tackle are 2 points aft of the beam on the spider bands
  4. Quarter tackle are 1 point on the stern.
  5. stay sails downhauls are aft on spider bands

Compass points:

  • points referred to bow and stern are "x point(s) on the { Bow | Stern }"
  • points referred to the beam are "x point(s) { Forward | Abaft } the { Port | Starboard } Beam}"
  • the 45 degrees are "Broad on the { Port | Starboard } { Bow | Quarter}"
