Abbreviated Terminology

line in hand take off pin and pay attention, expect line to be somewhat slack
down to one turn unbelay except for last wrap, line is under tension
watch let run keeping an eye on potential tangles
wait be listening for what to do, keep a line in hand if it was
sweat belay sweat the line and belay, IE really tighten the line
taut/tight/snug belay pull out slack but do NOT sweat, then belay
slack belay leave line a little bit slack, belay
stack the yards make your yard line up with course (track one below)
